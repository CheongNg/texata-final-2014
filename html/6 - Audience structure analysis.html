Audience structure analysis¶

We are interseted in validating the assumption about there being "mainly askers" and "mainly answerers" on the forum. Obviously, one way to do that is examining the question/answer stats of the users. For simplicity we'll only focus on the RS forum.

from sqlalchemy import func
from collections import Counter
user_reply_count = Counter(dict(DBSession.query(Reply.user_id, func.count(Reply.id)).group_by(Reply.user_id).all()))
user_question_count = Counter(dict(DBSession.query(Post.user_id, func.count(Post.id)).group_by(Post.user_id).all()))
user_vote_count = Counter(dict(DBSession.query(Post.user_id, func.sum(Post.vote_count)).group_by(Post.user_id).all()))
from tx.db import Engine
replies = Engine.execute("select reply.user_id, count(reply.id) from reply left join post on reply.post_id = post.id where reply.user_id <> post.user_id group by reply.user_id")
user_help_count = Counter(dict(list(replies)))
